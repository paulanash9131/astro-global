5 · Words We Use, Words We Never UseFacebook and Google will ban us if we get this wrong. This is the most important table.

✅ SAY THIS ⛔ NEVER SAY THIS
Talk about astrology in general: "In astrology, your rising sign shows how people first see you." Never tell the reader their own star sign: "As a Scorpio, you have been betrayed." ← Facebook bans this fast.
Ask about a life event: "Thinking about changing jobs?" Never say you know how they feel or what money problems they have: "Feeling worried?" "Short of money?"
Let them type in their birth details AFTER they click. Then the reading becomes personal. Never promise a result: "Get your ex back", "100% correct", "You will get rich".
Use gentle words: clarity, guidance, understanding, direction. Never talk about curing or healing: "heal your pain", "cure your worry". This is treated as a medical claim and it is illegal.
Show his real training papers, real reviews and real prices. Never say a reading or a stone helps someone win money or the lottery. This is banned in the whole of the UK and Europe.
Say what they get: how long the call is, what it costs, who he is. Never use fake countdown timers, fake "only 2 left", or fake reviews. In the UK the fine can be 10% of everything the company earns worldwide.
