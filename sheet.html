5 · The Ad & Copy RulesBreak these and the accounts get banned. Pulled from live, running astrology ads.

✅ ALWAYS DO THIS ⛔ NEVER DO THIS
Describe the SYSTEM in third person: "In astrology, your rising sign is the mask you wear." Name their sign AT them: "As a Scorpio, you've been betrayed." ← instant ban
Ask about a SITUATION: "Navigating a career decision?" Imply you know their state: "Feeling anxious?" "Struggling with money?"
Let the QUIZ personalise it — they hand over birth details AFTER the click Guarantee outcomes: "bring your ex back", "100% accurate", "manifest $10k"
Hedge softly: clarity, guidance, insight, direction Any healing/medical framing: "heal your trauma", "cure your anxiety"
Show real credentials, real review counts, real prices Lottery/gambling luck claims — banned outright across ALL of UK + EU
Sell the service, the duration, the price, the credential Fake urgency, fake scarcity, fake reviews — UK fines up to 10% of global turnover
