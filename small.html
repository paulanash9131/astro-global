The $10–20K/month math at real prices: at the verified US median of $150–200/session, $10K/month = 50–65 readings (~2–3/day) — feasible solo but saturating. That's exactly why the documented six-figure operators all shifted the excess demand into courses, digital products or subscriptions — and why our Roadmap builds async products and subscriptions from day one instead of discovering this ceiling in month six.

How they actually get clients — documented channel evidence

📱 Short-form content → booking funnel (the winning channel)

  • Maren Altman: TikTok content → 1M followers → $200/15-min premium pricing + $1,500 course. The documented ceiling case for content-led acquisition.
  • Starcrossed: viral astrology TikToks → app installs (self-reported $60–70K MRR, weakly sourced but directionally consistent).
  • Hint: pays TikTok influencers at scale — one documented campaign: 200K landing views → 1,500+ paying subscribers (single-source case study).

🔍 SEO/content → email → products

  • Biddy Tarot: 12M visits/yr content library feeding certification courses — the 7-figure blueprint.
  • Cafe Astrology: the same model at media scale (see Mid-Size).

🏪 Marketplaces (volume, low margin — gig rails)

  • Keen: advisors set $1.99–15/min, most $5–15/min — and keep ~46% of billings (Keen's own earnings page; split single-source). Glassdoor estimate for full-time advisors ~$109K/yr (n=5, weak).
  • Purple Garden: $0.99–17.99/min, avg ~$4.99/min.
  • Fiverr: gigs $10–45 (category norm $30–35), Fiverr takes 20% — $10K/mo needs ~360 gigs; it's a lead-gen tier, not a destination.

🧾 Etsy — the digital-report volume game

  • Chayathemedium: 36,000+ sales at ~$8.43–24.78/reading → ≥$300K lifetime gross floor (computed from public sale counts — marked assumption); 5,000+ listings in the category.
  • MarinaTeke: ~2,500 sales in 3 years ≈ $700–1,700/mo average (computed floor).
  • Format: cheap PDF readings delivered in 24h. Proof that the $8–75 async-report SKU sells at volume — but it's a different business than $200 consulting.
The takeaway that shapes our plan: every documented operator who broke past $10K/mo owns their audience (TikTok/SEO/email/Patreon) instead of renting marketplace traffic at a 46–54% platform tax. Our stack starts with owned channels + paid ads feeding an owned list — marketplaces are ignored entirely.
