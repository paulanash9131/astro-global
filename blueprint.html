🎥 The content engine — knowledge-led

Week 8 onwards · never stops

🚦 CHECKPOINT — Day 90

We don't scale until all four are true: winning a client costs under 40% of the sale · 1 in 4 return within 60 days · refunds under 5% · 10 real reviews live. Fail two, and we fix the business instead of buying more traffic.

💰 Paid ads — small & surgical

Month 3+ · only after the checkpoint

The honest truth: the big platforms pay others $75–175 to bring them a client, and buying that client on Google costs $109–182. Ads barely break even here — so they're an accelerator, never the engine.

  • Google Search first — buying intent, not curiosity
  • "vedic astrologer consultation" ✅   "daily horoscope" ❌
  • Then Microsoft/Bing (cheaper, older, richer)
  • Facebook last, once something already sells
  • Separate card. Second admin. (explained just below)
Start ~$60/day · never scale on a hunch

💳 "Separate card" — what & why

A bank card used only to pay for ads — nothing else, ever.

  • Google and Facebook link accounts by their shared payment card
  • If one account is banned, every other account on the same card can be banned with it
  • A dedicated card fences off that risk from the rest of the business

👥 "Second admin" — what & why

A second trusted person with admin access to the ad accounts.

  • If the main person's login gets locked, the accounts can die with it
  • A second admin keeps them running and can file the appeal
  • Costs nothing, set up in 5 minutes, saves the whole ad operation
Stop 1 in detail

The website — one brand, three countries

We build one site that adapts. And the free entry point is not just a birth-chart calculator — that mostly attracts people already into astrology. We lead with the questions everyone has, which pulls a far wider age range across all three countries.

🇺🇸 United States

Biggest market, highest prices.

  • Prices in USD, no conversion at checkout
  • Slots in Eastern & Pacific time
  • Recording consent is a legal must — a crime without it in several states
  • Never imply we know a visitor's health, money or beliefs

🇬🇧 United Kingdom

Strictest ad rules of the three.

  • Prices in GBP, VAT-ready
  • No fake discounts, urgency or reviews — fines reach 10% of worldwide turnover
  • We must hold certification for the fortune-telling / astrology services before any claim goes live
  • 14-day cancel rights → the "start now" tick-box

🇨🇦 Canada

Easiest to enter, US-like buyer.

  • Prices in CAD — never make them do the maths
  • Strictest email rules anywhere: consent, our postal address, instant unsubscribe
  • If a client refuses recording, we still serve them
  • Quebec has extra data rules — worth knowing early
The free entry point, broadened: a short "your big question" path — love, money, career, the year ahead — with the birth chart as one route inside it. It captures an email from a wide audience; the emails, not the ads, book the readings. This is the astro.com model that has worked for 40 years, opened up beyond the astrology crowd.

Stop 7 in detail · our unique selling point

The Prescribed Stone

Every other seller treats a gemstone as a product on a shelf. We make it the physical anchor of a relationship — and that is something nobody else offers. It's a service-and-product hybrid, so it never competes on stone price with ordinary crystal shops.

How it works, end to end

  • Matched to the chart — chosen for the client's real chart inside a paid reading, never sold cold
  • Independently certified — a third-party lab proves it's genuine and untreated
  • Registered to the client — a unique ID + printed certificate ties the stone to their chart in our registry. It's their stone, on record
  • Personally explained — a short recorded video: why this stone for this chart
  • Follow-up built in — a check-in reading 60–90 days later comes with it

Why it beats a normal shop

A plain stone competes on price with every crystal seller online. A chart-matched, certified, registered, personally-prescribed stone with a follow-up reading competes with nobody — because nobody offers it.

  • Justifies a premium price
  • Brings the client back for the follow-up
  • Turns a one-time sale into an ongoing relationship — where the real money is

Not pure order-on-demand: we hold a curated stock of the common chart-relevant stones so most orders ship fast; rare or bespoke stones are sourced to order.

  • The stone, in a proper jewellery box
  • The independent lab certificate (genuine, untreated)
  • A "Your Stone & Your Chart" card — why this stone, in the language of tradition
  • Traditional first-wearing guidance (day & finger, per Vedic custom)
  • A care card + a tamper-evident seal
✕ No health claims✕ Never "scientific"✕ Never "cure / heal / align"
The one hard line on stones: we sell jewellery, tradition and genuine certification — nothing more. Nothing in the box, on the site, or in any reading says a stone treats, heals, cures or improves any condition of body or mind. UK law bans "helps with," not just "cures," and there is no scientific proof to stand on — so we never claim any. This is a true, honest story, and it's all we ever need.
