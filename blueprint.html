🎥 The content engine

Week 8 onwards · never stops

This is the real growth engine, and it's free. Every small astrologer who broke past $10K/month did it with video, not ads.

  • His face, 45–90 seconds, answering one real question
  • 4–5 a week → TikTok + Reels + Shorts, same clips
  • 7 teach · 2 prove · 1 sell — never sell hard on camera
  • Every video ends: "free birth chart at our site"

He records 10 in one sitting · we do the rest

🚦 CHECKPOINT — Day 90

We do not scale until all four are true: cost to win a client is under 40% of the sale · 1 in 4 clients come back within 60 days · refunds under 5% · 10 real reviews live. Fail two, and we fix the business instead of buying more traffic.

💰 Paid ads — small and surgical

Month 3+ · only after the checkpoint

The uncomfortable truth: the big platforms pay others $75–175 to bring them a client, and buying that client on Google costs $109–182. Ads barely break even here. So they are an accelerator, never the engine.

  • Google Search only at first — buying words, not curious words
  • "vedic astrologer consultation" ✅   "daily horoscope" ❌
  • Then Microsoft/Bing (cheaper, older, richer audience)
  • Facebook last, and only once something already sells
  • Separate card. Second admin. Verified early.
Start $60/day · never scale on a hunch

Subscriptions keep trust — they can't create it. So this only opens once people already love his readings.

💎 Gemstones & remedies

Months 6–12

AstroTalk's stone shop went from zero to ~$24M/year in 14 months. Ordinary crystal shops make $2–5M. The whole difference: the astrologer recommends the stone inside a paid reading, and the shop sends it.

  • Order-on-demand first — no stock, no risk
  • Every stone ships with a real lab certificate
  • Sold as jewellery and tradition — never as medicine
  • Tell the bank before we start. Never add it quietly.

🇺🇸 United States

Our biggest market and highest prices.

  • Prices in USD, no conversion at checkout
  • Slots shown in Eastern and Pacific time
  • Recording consent is a legal must — several states make it a crime without it
  • Never claim to know a visitor's health, money or beliefs

🇬🇧 United Kingdom

Strictest advertising rules of the three.

  • Prices in GBP, VAT-ready
  • No fake discounts, no fake urgency, no fake reviews — fines reach 10% of worldwide turnover
  • We must hold proof of his credentials before any claim goes live
  • 14-day cancel rights → the "start now" tick-box

🇨🇦 Canada

Easiest to enter, US-like buyer.

  • Prices in CAD — never make them do the maths
  • Email rules are the strictest anywhere: consent, our postal address, instant unsubscribe
  • If a client refuses recording, we must still serve them
  • Quebec has extra data rules — worth knowing early
What it takes

The resources we need

People, tools and money — mapped to the stop where each one is actually needed. Nothing gets bought before it earns its place.

👥 People

$900–2,800/month
  • Helper #1 — from stop 2. Bookings, messages, collecting birth details, sending recordings.
  • Helper #2 — from stop 5. Reports, reviews, subscription admin, refunds.
  • Video editor — from stop 4. Cuts his raw clips into 4–5 posts a week.
  • Golden rule: helpers never give readings. "Let me book that with our astrologer."

🧰 Tools

$120–300/month
  • Booking: Calendly or Acuity — pays at booking, handles time zones
  • Calls: Zoom, paid tier (needed for recording)
  • Money: Stripe + a backup processor + PayPal
  • Inbox & email: one shared inbox; Brevo for email (EU-hosted, simpler privacy)
  • Phone: a business number + WhatsApp Business

💵 Money

$9K–27Kto reach day 90
  • Slow & safe: ~$2,900/month → about $9K to the checkpoint
  • Fast: ~$8,800/month → about $27K
  • Always keep 4–6 weeks of income in the bank in case a processor holds funds
  • My advice: go slow. Since ads barely break even, the patient route is also the more likely one to work.

🎓 What we need from your partner

  • His certification documents — before any claim goes on the site (UK law)
  • His past client list — this is our first 15–25 sales, for free
  • An honest number: how many readings a week can he do without burning out? Every plan depends on this.
  • His face on camera, weekly. Non-negotiable — he is the product.
  • Sign-off that we may add other astrologers later.

🛡️ Guard rails — never break these

  • Never promise a result. No "your ex returns", no guaranteed money, no curing anxiety.
  • Never fake anything — not a discount, not a countdown, not a review.
  • Never a "free" reading that quietly bills them. This destroyed Miss Cleo and got Nebula sued.
  • Never record without a spoken "yes" on the recording itself.
  • Never sell a stone as medicine. Jewellery and tradition only.
