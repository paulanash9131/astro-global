The idea in one line

What this business is

A premium online astrology consulting business. Real people get a real reading from a qualified astrologer — by video, by phone, or as a written and recorded report — and can stay with us through monthly plans and a matched, certified gemstone. We serve the US, UK and Canada in one brand.

The value is the knowledge and the study of the subject, not any single person's fame. Readings are delivered by qualified astrologers; educational content is delivered by presenters. The business owns the method, the brand and the client relationships — so it never depends on one face and can grow to many astrologers, many presenters, and many languages.

Why we win where others get shut down: every company destroyed in this field died the same way — a fake "free" offer, a promised outcome, or a billing trick. We simply never do those three things. That single discipline is our biggest advantage and it costs us nothing.

The offer · part 1

Reading prices

Whole, round numbers — never prices ending in .99 or 9. In this field, round numbers read as a professional fee; charm prices read as a discount store and quietly cheapen a premium astrologer. These figures are a starting point, set to rise as reputation builds.

ReadingLengthPriceWhat's included

Full Reading ⭐ most booked60 min$195Three life areas. Live video + recording + a short written summary.
Deep Dive90 min$325Full chart + a 12-month outlook. Live + recording + written summary + one follow-up question.

Positioning note: the competitor field is jammed at $300–$360, and the best-known independents are booked months out — a clear sign they are underpriced. As reviews build, Full Reading can move to $225 and Deep Dive to $375–$400. UK shown in GBP, Canada in CAD, at the equivalent local level — never a currency conversion at checkout.

The middle tier is designed to win. Three real choices are always shown together. The 60-minute Full Reading is the best value-per-minute and looks it, so most people land there. We never show a single lonely price — a price with nothing to compare against is compared only to zero.

The offer · part 3

Founders' Circle (one-time, lifetime) + Fair Usage Policy

The deal: 150 seats, $1,000 once, kept for life. Sold in two honest waves (50 then 100) with a real, visible counter. About $150,000 of launch capital that funds the gemstone line and the second astrologer — with no extra share of the company given away.

Each founder gets: lifetime Monthly-Guidance content · 3 live 45-minute sessions (to use within 24 months) · a permanent 20% off all readings and store purchases · founder status and first access to everything new.

Fair Usage Policy — so nobody abuses it
  • Fixed sessions, not unlimited: 3 sessions inside 24 months. They cannot be stockpiled, carried on forever, or claimed all at once.
  • Reasonable-use limit on monthly questions — one per month, not a flood.
  • Personal use only. One person, one account. No sharing, reselling, or gifting the seat or the sessions.
  • Booking with fair notice, subject to normal availability — no last-minute demands.
  • Format may evolve: we may change how the lifetime content is delivered over time, so we're never locked to one method forever.
  • Abuse ends access: reselling, fraud or repeated breaches can revoke the lifetime access.
Our signature — the thing nobody else does

The Prescribed Stone

Every other seller treats a gemstone as a product on a shelf. We make it the physical anchor of a relationship. This is our unique selling point and our moat.

How it works, end to end:

  • Matched to the chart. The stone is chosen for the client's actual birth chart inside a paid reading — never sold cold.
  • Independently certified. Every stone ships with a third-party lab certificate proving it is genuine and untreated.
  • Registered to the client. Each stone gets a unique ID and a printed certificate tying it to their chart in our registry. It is their stone, on record.
  • Personally explained. A short recorded video explains why this stone for this chart, and the traditional way to begin wearing it.
  • Built-in follow-up. A check-in reading 60–90 days later is included — the stone brings them back.
Why this beats a normal shop

A plain stone competes on price with every crystal seller online. A chart-matched, certified, registered, personally-prescribed stone with a follow-up reading competes with nobody — because nobody offers it. It justifies a premium price, it drives clients back for the follow-up, and it turns a one-time sale into an ongoing relationship. That is where the real money in this field actually is.

We do not run it as pure order-on-demand. We hold a curated stock of the common chart-relevant stones so most orders ship fast; rare or bespoke stones are sourced to order.

Shipping

  • Always fully insured, tracked, and signature-required — these are high-value items with certificates.

What goes in the box

  • The stone, in a proper jewellery box.
  • The independent lab certificate (genuine, untreated).
  • A "Your Stone & Your Chart" card — why this stone for this chart, in the language of tradition.

What NEVER goes in the box

No leaflet, card, or wording that says the stone treats, heals, cures, aligns, fixes, or improves any condition of body or mind. No "scientifically proven." No health promise of any kind. The stone is jewellery and tradition — that is the whole story we tell, and it is a true one.

The line we never cross

The claims rulebook

This applies to everyone — astrologers, presenters and support staff — everywhere: the website, the readings, the videos, the ads and the packaging. When unsure, say less.

Gemstones & remedies

✅ We may say⛔ We never say
In Vedic tradition, this stone is associated with Jupiter.This stone heals / cures / treats / fixes anything.
It's what your chart indicates, in the traditional system.It helps you recover faster / improves your health / aligns your body.
The stone is genuine and untreated — here is the lab certificate.It's scientifically proven. (There is no such proof — saying it is itself the violation.)
Traditionally worn on this finger, first put on this day.Wear it and your money / relationship / illness will improve.
Where the law actually draws the line: UK rules ban claiming a product can treat "any adverse condition of body or mind." Not just "cure" — helps with is already over the line. A crystal seller was ruled against for a generic "healing power" claim with no illness named at all. So the rule is simple: no health claims of any kind, and never the word "scientific." We sell jewellery, tradition and genuine certification — nothing more, and we never need more.

Non-negotiable

The guard rails

Always
  • Show the exact price before anyone pays.
  • Put a cancel link in every receipt; send a reminder before every charge; allow one-click cancel.
  • Get a spoken "yes" on the recording before recording any call. If they say no, stop and continue unrecorded.
  • Refund quickly and politely when asked.
  • Keep card complaints under 4 in every 1,000 sales.
Never
  • Promise a result of any kind.
  • Fake a discount, a countdown, a shortage or a review.
  • Offer a "free" reading that quietly turns into a bill.
  • Push payment plans on someone in distress. If a client describes a crisis, we point them to real help — we do not sell them a reading.
  • Sell a stone as medicine.

The two safety habits, explained. Separate card: the ad accounts use one bank card used for nothing else — because Google and Facebook link accounts by their card, so if one gets banned, others on the same card can be banned too.
