Who the buyer is

Three independent datasets (Pew, YouGov, AMR) converge on the same target profile. Verified 3-0.

Women 18–49 — the primary segment

  • 46% of US women 18–49 consult astrology/tarot at least annually (Pew)
  • 43% of women 18–49 believe in astrology vs 16% of men 50+ (Pew)
  • Women are ~2× as likely as men to believe: 35% vs 18% (Pew); YouGov corroborates US ~39% vs ~21%, UK 42% vs 19%

Millennials — the dominant generation

  • Dominant end-user generation globally in 2021, ~36% share (per AMR), projected to stay dominant through 2031
  • LSN Global survey (Feb 2024): millennials lead astrology interest — 83% believers vs 62% of Gen Z
  • EduBirdie survey of 2,000 young Americans (via Fortune, Aug 2024): ~80% of Gen Z + millennials believe in astrology — single survey, attributed as such

LGBT adults — the highest-index segment

  • 54% consult at least yearly — ~2× the 28% all-adult rate (Pew)
  • 21% rely on it at least a little for major decisions (Pew)

Young adults

  • 13% of 18–29s rely on astrology/tarot at least a little for major life decisions (Pew)
  • Belief levels nationally are stable, not exploding: 29% (2017) → 27% (2024) — the "astrology boom" is a distribution/monetization boom, not a belief boom (Pew)

Sources: Pew May 2025 · YouGov US/UK belief surveys · AMR · Fortune, Aug 2024

Service types in the market

The commercial service landscape as it actually exists across platforms and independents. (AMR's claim that "love astrology is the largest service segment" was refuted 1-2 in verification — no reliable service-mix split exists, so none is stated.)

Live consultation

1:1 natal chart, compatibility/synastry, career, timing ("when will X happen") — delivered by video call, phone, or per-minute chat. The core of every $MM/mo business in this niche.

Written & recorded

Birth-chart reports, year-ahead forecasts, recorded video readings, voice-note readings. Higher margin, asynchronous, no calendar constraint.

Systems

Western (psychological, evolutionary), Vedic/Jyotish (predictive, remedy-oriented — gemstones live here), Chinese, tarot-adjacent. Vedic is the natural fit for a remedies/gemstone commerce arm.
